Our Cookie Policy:

CSGOCranked.com, like most other websites, may store or retrieve information on your browser. This is done in the form of 'cookies'.


Below, you can find out more about the different cookies we use.


Necessary Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable you to navigate around the site, remember your choices, and enable a smooth user experience. Without these cookies we would not be able to provide the services that you come to the site for.


Performance Cookies

These cookies allow us to understand how users utilise our site. This lets us measure statistics, and improve how the site works. For example, they help us to know which skins are the most, and least popular. They are responsible for counting things like the number of visitors, how long people spend on the site, and user stats. This lets us know what users like, and where we can improve, as well as making sure the pages load quickly, smoothly, and display as intended.


Cookies

These cookies are used to enable us to provide enhanced functionality. This includes the ‘Free Tokens’ page functionality, providing information about skin prices, and also allowing you to share content through social media. These services are mostly provided by third parties. If you have an account, or use the services of these parties on other websites then they may be able to know that you have visited our site. The use of data collected through cookies by those third parties is subject to their own privacy policies. Therefore, we identify these cookies by the parties setting them.


Opting Out of Cookies

f you have any concerns about our use of cookies, you can take action to prevent them being set in the first place. Methods of prevention include changing your browser settings to block certain types of cookies.
